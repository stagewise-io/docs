Open account settings at **Settings → Account**.

## Signing in

stagewise uses passwordless email authentication:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type your email address in the sign-in form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive OTP">
    A one-time password (OTP) is sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Enter the 6-digit OTP to complete authentication.
  </Step>
</Steps>

No password is needed. Each sign-in generates a fresh OTP.

## Account details

Once signed in, the account page displays:

- **Email** — Your registered email address
- **Plan** — Current subscription tier (e.g. Free, Pro)
- **Status** — Whether the subscription is active
- **Expiration** — When the current billing period ends
- **Machine ID** — A unique identifier for this device

## Usage tracking

The usage section shows your current consumption across rate-limit windows:

- **Credits** — Remaining prepaid balance (displayed as a dollar amount)
- **Daily / Weekly / Monthly windows** — Percentage of your rate limit consumed in each window, with reset timers

Usage bars show color-coded status:
- Green — Under 80% consumed
- Yellow — Between 80–100% consumed
- Red — Rate limit reached

## Console

Click **Open Console** to open the [stagewise console](https://console.stagewise.io) in a new tab. The console provides full account management including billing, subscription changes, and detailed usage history.

## Signing out

Click **Sign out** to deauthenticate this device. Your settings, workspaces, and chat history are preserved locally — signing back in restores access to proxy-based inference.

## Using stagewise without an account

You can use stagewise without signing in by configuring [BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)](/agent/byok-setup). In BYOK mode, requests go directly to the provider's API using your key — no stagewise account needed.
